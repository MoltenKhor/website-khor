Hey, I'm Marco


Computer Scientist who loves all about tech.
Since 2015 i started building my own computers, i ended up building other's keyboards, and i love it.

Passion


In 2019 i started making cables for my keyboards, people on socials liked it so much that ended up being my main hobby. In 2021 Khor became an established business and since then my goal has been to offer the best mechanical keyboard experience to all Italians and worldwide.

Perfection


The continuous research for improvement is what makes every build near perfection, that's why i can't stop building new ones.
Building a keyboard is a hobby, building a perfect keyboard is art.
